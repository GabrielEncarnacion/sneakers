About Us

Indianolas go to sneaker store

We are a local startup specializing in exclusive sneakers and want to do our best to put you in a shoe you can't get anywhere else. We are college student athletes that have a passion for shoes and want to help others with the same passion get the pairs they need. If you are a fellow classmate or alumni we will cut you a deal just send us proof. We operate out of the Kresge dorms and can ship shoes anywhere in the US.
